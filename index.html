Welcome to TCG Tracker for Pokemon!

TCG Tracker allows you to search for all your favourite Pokemon cards. It allows you to add them to a collection to keep track of cards you own. You can also view and sort your collection by type and HP. You can also build up to four decks from your collection, no more searching through binders to find the cards you need; and if your a completionist check out the series to see what cards your missing from a set!
